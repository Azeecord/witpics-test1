What is smart Security

Smart security refers to the integration of advanced technologies, such as artificial intelligence, internet of things (IoT), and automation, into traditional security systems to enhance safety, protection,
and convenience. This intelligent and interconnected approach to security has revolutionized the way we safeguard our homes, businesses, and public spaces.

But how all these works??


smart security works through the integration of advanced technologies, networking, and intelligent algorithms to enhance safety,
monitoring, and control in various settings.
